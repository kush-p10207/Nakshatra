About Nakshatra

Nakshatra is the Astronomy & Mathematics Society of NSUT, uniting students who are passionate about unraveling the mysteries of the cosmos and the elegance of mathematics. We organize stargazing nights, lectures, workshops, and more to foster curiosity and learning.
